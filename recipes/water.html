Water

Description:

Water is the common name for dihydrogen monoxide or H2O. The molecule is produced from numerous chemical reactions, including the synthesis reaction from its elements, hydrogen, and oxygen.

Ingredients:

  • Hydrogen gas
  • Oxygen gas

Steps:

  1. Mix the two gases together.
  2. Add a spark or sufficient heat to provide the activation energy to start the reaction.
  3. Energy must be supplied to break the covalent bonds that hold H2 and O2 molecules together.
